About Us

SPS National

The Society of Physics Students is a professional association within the American Institute of Physics. It is designed explicitly to ensure the professional success of those with interests in the fields of physics. Our Mission

Our Mission, as written in our Constitution, is:

  • To encourage all students interested in the Fields of Physics
  • To facilitate the professional development of all members
  • To advance the local communityâ€™s interest in Physics
  • To develop a community within the student body who themselves demonstrate the core values of SPS
We seek to carry out this mission through a combination of regularly-scheduled meetings and extemporaneous projects designed to contribute to personal and professional development; these often taking the form of invited speakers and student workshops, as well as campus and community service events. When combined with our unique culture, they serve to make our organization a central figure in the campus life of physics majors during their time at Stony Brook University.
