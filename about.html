About Us

SPS National

The Society of Physics Students is a professional association within the American Institute of Physics. It is designed explicitly to ensure the professional success of those with interests in the fields of physics. Stony Brook Chapter

The Stony Brook Chapter of the Society of Physics Students aims to foster the academic, professional, and social growth of its members by:

  • Providing a welcoming undergraduate support network for an active, engaged, and diverse membership
  • Disseminating physics research, especially that conducted at Stony Brook University, through undergraduate level lectures and demonstrations
  • Advancing career development by hosting training workshops and promoting opportunities for success
  • Encouraging interest in physics in our local communities through effective physics education
