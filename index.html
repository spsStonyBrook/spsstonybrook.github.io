Welcome to SPS Stony Brook

Our chapter continually serves our community by increasing engagement and efficacy in physics. We run outreach programs, collaborative workshops, and host talks by students and professors alike. Our primary goal is to build a community for those interested in physics here at Stony Brook University where they can connect with like-minded individuals from a wide variety of backgrounds, and make friends for life. This website features a number of resources which we've compiled for the benefit of our members here, and anyone else who might come across them.
